# Privacy Policy for WantBoard

**Last Updated: December 28, 2025**

## Introduction

WantBoard ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application WantBoard (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

When you register for and use our App, we collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

- **Email Address**: Used for account creation and authentication
- **Name**: Your display name for your profile and to identify list owners
- **User Content**: Wishlists you create, including:
  - List names, descriptions, and icons
  - Item names, URLs, prices, and notes
  - Budget information
  - Share codes and collaboration settings

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

- **User ID**: A unique identifier assigned by Firebase Authentication to manage your account
- **Product Interaction Data**: Information about how you interact with the App, including:
  - App opens and session duration
  - Features you use (creating lists, adding items, sharing)
  - Button clicks and navigation patterns
  - Error logs and performance data
- **Purchase History**: Information about your subscription status (free or premium)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:
- Phone numbers
- Physical addresses
- Location data (GPS coordinates)
- Photos or videos
- Health or fitness data
- Financial information (credit card details are handled by Apple)
- Device advertising identifiers
- Contacts from your phone

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

### Provide App Functionality
- Create and manage your account
- Store and sync your wishlists across devices
- Enable list sharing and collaboration features
- Process premium subscription upgrades
- Provide customer support

### Improve Our Services
- Analyze how users interact with the App
- Identify and fix bugs
- Measure app performance
- Plan new features based on usage patterns

### Security and Fraud Prevention
- Authenticate users and prevent unauthorized access
- Detect and prevent fraudulent activities
- Ensure the security of our services

## How We Share Your Information

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### Service Providers
We share your information only with trusted service providers who help us operate the App:

- **Firebase (Google)**: Authentication, database storage, and analytics
  - [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **RevenueCat**: Subscription management and in-app purchase processing
  - [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
- **Apple**: Payment processing (we never see your payment details)
  - [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as:
- Court orders or subpoenas
- Government investigations
- Protection of our rights or safety of others

### Business Transfers
If WantBoard is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred to the new owners.

## Collaboration and Shared Lists

When you share a list with others:
- Other users will see your display name as the list owner
- Collaborators can view or edit the list based on permissions you grant
- Share codes are unique to each list and can be deactivated at any time

You control who has access to your lists and can remove collaborators at any time.

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.

When you delete your account:
- Your personal information is permanently deleted within 30 days
- Your wishlists and items are removed from our servers
- Shared lists you owned are deleted
- Some information may be retained in backup systems for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data
View all your personal information and wishlists within the App.

### Update Your Data
Edit your profile name and email address in the Profile screen.

### Delete Your Account
Permanently delete your account and all associated data through Settings > Delete Account in the App. This action cannot be undone.

### Manage Subscriptions
Cancel your premium subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- **Authentication**: Secure login via Firebase Authentication
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal data by our team
- **Regular Updates**: We keep our security measures up to date

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

WantBoard is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us through the App Store.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Tracking and Advertising

### We Do NOT Track You
We do not:
- Use your data for targeted advertising
- Share your data with advertising networks
- Link your data with data from other companies' apps or websites
- Sell your data to data brokers

### Analytics
We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. This helps us improve features and fix bugs. The analytics data is used solely for our own product development and is not shared with advertisers.

### No Ads
WantBoard does not display advertisements.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

- **Right to Know**: Request details about the personal information we collect
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt-out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell your data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the App Store.

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights:

- **Right of Access**: Obtain confirmation of whether we process your data
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate personal data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive your data in a structured format
- **Right to Object**: Object to processing of your data
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the App Store.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:
- We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top
- We will notify you via in-app notification for material changes
- Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
